About Dr. Bennett's Veterinary House Calls

Meet Dr. David Bennett, DVM

Dr. David Bennett is a compassionate, experienced veterinarian dedicated to providing dignified, end-of-life care for beloved pets in the comfort and familiarity of their homes. With years of experience in veterinary medicine, Dr. Bennett understands the profound bond between pets and their families.

Our Mission

At Dr. Bennett's Veterinary House Calls, our mission is to provide compassionate, professional, and stress-free in-home euthanasia services and veterinary care throughout Contra Costa County. We believe every pet deserves a peaceful, dignified goodbye surrounded by loved ones in the place they know and love.

Our Approach

We focus on creating a calm, supportive environment where families can say goodbye with confidence that their pet is receiving expert, compassionate care. Our in-home approach eliminates travel stress, allows for private family time, and ensures your pet's comfort is our top priority.

Services Offered

  • In-home pet euthanasia
  • Hospice and palliative care
  • General medicine consultations
  • Mobile veterinary care throughout Contra Costa County
