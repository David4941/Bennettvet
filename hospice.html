Pet Hospice & General Medicine Services

Compassionate Veterinary Care Beyond Euthanasia

While in-home pet euthanasia is our primary service, Dr. Bennett's Veterinary House Calls also offers compassionate hospice care and general medicine services. These services focus on your pet's comfort, quality of life, and well-being during their final stages of life.

Pet Hospice Care

Pet hospice is a compassionate approach to end-of-life care that prioritizes comfort and quality of life. When curative treatment is no longer the goal, hospice care shifts focus to managing pain, maintaining dignity, and supporting your family during this difficult transition.

What Hospice Care Includes:

  • Pain management and symptom control
  • Comfort measures and support
  • Guidance on quality of life assessments
  • Family support and counseling
  • Coordination of end-of-life care

General Medicine Services

Dr. Bennett provides mobile general medicine consultations and care for pets in Contra Costa County. Whether your pet needs a second opinion, chronic condition management, or wellness consultations, we bring professional veterinary care to your home.

General Medicine Services Include:

  • Health consultations and assessments
  • Chronic disease management
  • Wellness evaluations
  • Medical guidance and support
  • Coordination with specialists when needed

Why Choose Mobile Veterinary Care?

  • Reduced stress for pets who fear veterinary clinics
  • More personal, unhurried attention
  • Comfort in familiar home environment
  • Convenient scheduling for families
  • Professional, compassionate care delivered to your home

Our Approach to Care

Whether providing hospice support, general medicine consultations, or end-of-life euthanasia services, our focus remains on your pet's comfort and your family's peace of mind.
